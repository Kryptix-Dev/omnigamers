Our Winter Survey Results!

So long, and thanks for all the data!

  • Mar 11, 2018
  • by Kryptix

First I want to give a big thanks to everyone who took our survey! It's the longest one we've asked you to fill out, but it has given us a lot of data to parse through to better understand the community. Again, thank you! What I'll do to provide my insight into the results is go through each question and relay the consistent and interesting tidbits.

The first question is "Do you think the community is going in the right direction?" The good news is that everyone said yes, so yay! I'm glad to hear that everyone agrees things are going well! The second and third questions are about our Discord and the channels we have. We do have many channels, so I'm always concerned that there may be too many. 23% said there are too many. Unfortunately, those who said yes didn't provide any thoughts on what channels to remove, but I'll make sure not to get too crazy with adding more. There are four comments about our channels that struck me though. One asked to have a console gaming channel. I think we could try this and see how it goes.

RedW1dow is our Newest Creator!

A hearty salute to Red!

  • Mar 10, 2018
  • by Kryptix

I'm happy to announce our newest creator, RedW1dow! Red is a relatively new member, but has made a big impact since joining! Even to the point of earning the title "The Namer" for creating some unique names for our Discord channels! I'm happy to be bringing Red into the team! Below is a word from Red as well as a link to his Twitch channel. Make sure to subscribe!

My name is Red. I am a nerd. My video game history is a bit erratic. I played Super Mario World, MK1, MK2, and a few other games on SNES. From there I moved on to PSX where I played a lot of THPS and Spyro. On PS2, I played all the GTA's, COD's, MoH's, and Need for Speed's. From there I made a transition to Xbox 360. That's where I got hooked on multiplayer. I played a LOT of Halo, MW2, and Oblivion and Skyrim. I mainly play on Xbox and currently focus on Rainbow Six Siege and Fortnite. I tend to mainly stream between Friday afternoon Monday afternoons.
