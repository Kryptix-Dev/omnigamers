Rocket League & World of Warships in Feb!

Two weeks of two big games in February!

  • Jan 30, 2018
  • by Kryptix

Starting in February, OmniGamers is trying something new with our gaming events. Instead of one specific time during the day or night to meet up, we're trying a week long event! In February, we have two games scheduled, Rocket League and World of Warships! Our Rocket League week will begin this Saturday, Feb 3 with a kick-off event between 1-2:30 pm Central. The week long event will then continue to Friday Feb 9. World of Warships (which is free-to-play) will start Feb 18 and go to Feb 25.

New Player of the Week Crowned!

Foshiee fought the lag in TagPro!

  • Jan 19, 2018
  • by Kryptix

The biggest frustration with any gamer is lag. It's difficult to enjoy a game when everything you see is a second or more after it actually happened, and when your reactions also experience the same delay. This is what Foshiee struggled through with our matches in TagPro, but he persevered! Kudos to you Foshiee, for having the patience of a saint!

New Player of the Week Crowned!

TheRandomist survived the most in the Town of Salem!

  • Jan 5, 2018
  • by Kryptix

Surviving in the Town of Salem is not an easy task. Figuring out who is lying and who is telling the truth is critical. Knowing how to blend in when part of the mafia, or connecting the dots when finding the mafia is difficult in a text chat game, but TheRandomist proved himself to be a survivor and filtering out the lies. Congrats Randomist, on surviving to the end!
