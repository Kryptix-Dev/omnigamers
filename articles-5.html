Shoc is Our Player of the Week!

Bringing in that Precious Oil in World of Warships!

  • Feb 24, 2018
  • by Kryptix

This week's Player of the Week has been selected due to Shoc bringing in a LOT of oil for our clan! To date, Shoc has brought in 330 barrels since we created the clan on Feb 10! That is some dedication! Torres2131 is second at 320 and then myself at third with 300. For those unfamiliar with World of Warships, oil is obtained through opening up free daily crates and is used by the clan officers to research facilities to offer bonuses to the clan members. With a big push by everyone this last week in the clan, we were able to open the U.S. Research Center to offer all clan members with a U.S. ship an XP boost!

For those who are in the clan, please continuing playing and earning us oil so we can unlock more bonuses for everyone! Thank you for your hard work this week Shoc!

Special TagPro Event on Feb 24!

OmniGamers vs. VentureKraft TagPro Event!

  • Feb 20, 2018
  • by Kryptix

The gauntlet has been thrown! In our first joint event, OmniGamers will be going head-to-head against VentureKraft in a special TagPro event! To get more information about the event, such as the time it is for you in your timezone, the invite link (which will also be posted in Discord), and a link to TagPro, please check our event calendar here.

For those unfamiliar, VentureKraft is the name of the Minecraft server community that took over after I stepped down as owner of OmniGamers, which is the inspiration for the OmniGamers name! As is usual for our events, there will be an invite link provided about an hour before the event starts.
