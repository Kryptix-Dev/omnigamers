Join the OG Crew for a community that supports any game!

The OG Crew Discord started up to continue the friendship that many of us built up during the time that OmniKraft closed, and new leadership took over to continue as VentureKraft. At first, we formed to provide events for the VentureKraft community, but realized that the interest in games expanded past Minecraft for the long-term members. That's when OmniGamers started, to build on that interest in other games.

From games, we built up our Discord server to also cover topics in technology, history, cooking, music and cars! We're not like other places that just focus on one or two topics, we want to have conversations on any topic our members are interested in! We're open to new ideas and perspectives to broaden our own and welcome anyone from any background!
