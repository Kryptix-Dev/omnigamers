OG vs. VK 2 - Flags with a Vengeance

OmniGamers Dominating VentureKraft!

  • Apr 1, 2018
  • by Kryptix

Thank you everyone for joining round 2 of the friendly feud between OmniGamers and VentureKraft! We had a great showing of support for this event, to the point that not everyone could play as we maxed out the teams! Thank you for showing up! Of course, the real reason you're here is you want to know who won. In a clear show of dominance, OmniGamers beat VentureKraft in 8 out of 11 games! Below are the stats:

Top scores (in descending order):
TheRandomist (414), Kryptix (389), SuperSheep (341), Garrett (340), iToxic (328), Shasnas (248), Caldera (242), brjedi (160)

MVPs per game (in ascending order):
SuperSheep (VK), Caldera (OG), Caldera (OG), Kryptix (OG), TheRandomist (OG), Garrett (OG), Shasnas (VK), Kryptix (OG), Garrett (OG), Shasnas (VK), iToxic (VK)

With this, TheRandomist is OmniGamers Player of the Week! Being the top scorer out of all the games means being consistent, and TheRandomist was the most consistent player in all the matches! Thank you again everyone for joining!

Make sure to sign up and join us for our next OG vs. VK event to see if VK can upset OG! The current standings are: OG 2 - VK 0. Perhaps next time, VK can give us a run for our money!
