Events Scheduled for March!

Check out what we have planned for March!

  • Feb 28, 2018
  • by Kryptix

Continuing from our commitment from the beginning of the year, we are scheduling events for the next month at the end of the current month to make it easier for everyone to work with their schedule! It has made a difference in attendance and enjoyment when we have longer events than a one time event over a few hours!

You can use the links below to see the details for each scheduled game. Take note that the Rocket League tournament has a signup cutoff to gauge how many will show up so we can determine if the tournament will be singles or doubles.
Fortnite, Brawlhalla, OG vs VK TagPro, Payday 2, Rocket League, and a Rocket League Tournament!

We also have scheduled our first town hall meeting for the community! This is an open meeting with our Coordinators to discuss topics regarding the community that everyone can join! While voice will only be allowed for the Coordinators (to avoid disruption), a text channel will be available for everyone to share their comments and suggestions.

We've also opened our quarterly survey for the community to let us know what games you're playing so we know what to focus on for future events, and feedback for the community as a whole to figure out if we should make any changes. You can go to our survey here: https://goo.gl/forms/X8UZdzyzsryaCO5h2
The more of you who fill out the survey, the more info we have to make better decisions for you!

With our additional Coordinators, we're excited to not only schedule more game events in a month, but also offer more features such as our town hall meeting! We hope to see you there!
