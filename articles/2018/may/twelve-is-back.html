12.5 Makes a Comeback!

As Both a Creator and Coordinator!

  • May 2, 2018
  • by Kryptix

I'm happy to announce that TwelvePointFive has decided to jump back into the fray since life has calmed down for him! Not only is Twelve interested in running events, but he can also help us more by hosting servers as well! Look forward to some more events in June since we have both Twelve and Red who recently joined as well! Below is a statement that Twelve wanted to share!

“Hey everyone! I’m glad to be back in town as a Coordinator! I hope together we can have fun with good events and grow OmniGamers as the wonderful community it is! If anyone has anything they’d like to see happen or just wants to chat, feel free to talk to me. I’d love to help make your wants for this community into a reality!”
