First Rocket League Tournament!

And the outcome was completely unexpected!

  • Mar 26, 2018
  • by OriontheHunterr

The first ever OmniGamers Rocket League tournament started off with a bang this past weekend! We had seven participants compete in a grueling single eliminations tournament. Each match was best of three and every team gripped the pitch with all their cars could handle while trying to achieve victory!

Here are the rankings! In 4th place the team of Kryptix and CalderaTheArcance. In 3rd place was the team made up by OriontheHunterr and TheRandomist. 2nd place was taken by Garrett. For those of you that don’t know Garrett is probably the best Rocket League player in OmniGamers. So he had a bit of a handicap and played with a bot. And last by not least. The winner of the first ever OmniGamers Rocket League tournament was…

X3racer and Clumby! Because of their big win in the finals x3racer and Clumby are this weeks PLAYERS OF THE WEEK! Congratulations! You guys tried really hard and it paid off.

If you want to check out the fun, OriontheHunterr and TheRandomist both streamed the event for your viewing please. Also if you want to check out the bracket and match outcomes you can check it out at challonge.com/lprl9qju

Thank you to everyone for participating. We hope to do this again soon and make it an even larger tournament.
