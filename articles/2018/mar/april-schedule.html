Events Scheduled for April!

Check out what we have planned for April!

  • Mar 28, 2018
  • by Kryptix

As we come to Good Friday and Easter, OmniGamers is getting ready to make April another great month for gaming and community! We have something new for April! Not only a new game, but a multi-part schedule for this game. Civilization 5 has three parts to it's event due to how long the games take. We look forward to seeing how this event goes to consider similar longer form games!

We are also continuing our event with TagPro against the VentureKraft community! OmniGamers won the last event, so either join to help us continue our domination over VentureKraft or join to dethrone us! Either way, we look forward to seeing you there!

You can use the links below to see the details for each scheduled game.
Hedgewars, World of Warships, Civilization V Pt. 1, Minecraft Super Hostile, Civilization V Pt. 2, Civilization V Final, OG vs. VK TagPro

No town hall meeting for April. We'll try with a schedule of every other month to see how it goes for the amount of news shared and what the rest of the community thinks. As always, we look forward to seeing the turn out for our events grow! I don't know about you, but I tend to enjoy games more when there are more friends to enjoy the game with! If you have a friend that you'd like to invite to join these events, pass them along the website so they can see our calendar and jump into Discord!
